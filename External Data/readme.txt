This subfolder contains all the external datasets used in the climate change opinion prediction project.  Here are the files:

YCOM-2020-Data.csv               Climate Change Opinion data by county, from the Yale Climate Communication Project
co-est2019-alldata.csv           County population statistics 2010-2019, from the US Census Bureau
coastline-counties-list.xlsx     List of counties that border the coast, from the US Census Bureau
climdiv-pcpncy-v1.0.0-20201205   Precipitation history by county, from the US National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration
climdiv-tmpccy-v1.0.0-20201205   Temperature history by county, from the US National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration
climdiv-tmaxcy-v1.0.0-20201205   Maximum temperature history by county, from the US National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration
climdiv-tmincy-v1.0.0-20201205   Minimum temperature history by county, from the US National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration
CAGDP1_ALL_AREAS_2001_2019.csv   GDP by county, from the US Bureau of Economic Analysis
CAINC1_ALL_AREAS_1969_2019.csv   Income by county, from the US Bureau of Economic Analysis
dpr-data.xlsx                    Oil and natural gas production by region (counties defined), from the US Energy Information Administration
table2.xlsx                       Coal production by county, from the US Energy Information Administration
